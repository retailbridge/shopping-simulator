XR Shopping Simulator

A fast, light-hearted VR mini-game where you stand at a checkout counter, grab items, and scan them as quickly as you can before the clock hits zero.

Inspired by the spirit of Job Simulator, this demo focuses on hand-tracked grabbing, clean spatial UI, and a simple, replayable loop you can use for testing, showcasing, or just having a bit of fun.

Tip: Once the page has loaded, press "Enter VR" or "Start VR" from your browser to experience the game in your headset.
